Kashmir

Last summer, I finally had the chance to visit Kashmir. After postponing the trip by around 45 days, hoping we would come back alive, we were able to go — and trust me, it was the most beautiful place I have ever visited. After seeing Kashmir, I feel like I don't want to go anywhere else, because nothing could match its beauty. You could say that Kashmir made me fall in love with both the hills and itself.

Lucknow

So, Vasu’s random post-nap Lucknow plan led to ddp (Duke-Dard-Peeda)(our cringe WhatsApp group of five), heading to Lucknow.
